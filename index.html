Introduction

I am currently a PhD student in the Multimedia Information Computing Group of Center of Digital Media Information Processing at the School of Computer Science and Information Technology, Beijing Jiaotong University, directed by Yao Zhao and Shikui Wei. Now I am a visting student in the Center of Quantum Computation & Intelligent Systems, University of Technology Syndey, under the supervision of Yi Yang and Liang Zheng. My research areas are multimedia, computer vision and machine learning. I am interested in cross-media retrieval and image retrieval.
