Introduction

I am currently a PhD student in the Multimedia Information Computing Group (MIC) of Center of Digital Media Information Processing (MePro) at the School of Computer Science and Information Technology, Beijing Jiaotong University, directed by Yao Zhao and Shikui Wei. I used to be a visting student in the Center of Quantum Computation & Intelligent Systems, University of Technology Syndey, under the supervision of Yi Yang and Liang Zheng from Oct. 2016 to Oct. 2017. My research interests include multimedia retrieval, computer vision and deep learning. I will graduate in 2019, and now I'm looking for a job in computer vision.
